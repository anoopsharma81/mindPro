## ğŸ§  Creating Reflections

### The What/So What/Now What Framework

**1. What happened?**
- Describe the situation objectively
- Focus on your experience, not clinical details
- Use "a patient" or age ranges (e.g., "patient in their 60s")

**2. So what? (Analysis)**
- What did you learn?
- Why was this significant?
- What surprised or challenged you?

**3. Now what? (Action)**
- What will you do differently?
- What changes to your practice?
- What further learning do you need?

### Select GMC Domains
Choose which GMC Good Medical Practice domains your reflection addresses:
- **Domain 1**: Knowledge, Skills and Performance
- **Domain 2**: Safety and Quality
- **Domain 3**: Communication, Partnership and Teamwork
- **Domain 4**: Maintaining Trust

You can select multiple domains per reflection.

- Specific ward/bed locations

## ğŸ“ Tracking CPD

### Add CPD Activities
1. Click **CPD** on the dashboard
2. Click **+ button**
3. Select type:
   - Course
   - Reading
   - Audit
   - Teaching
   - Other
4. Enter **title**, **hours**, **date**, and **notes**
5. Save

### Filter & Analyze
- **Filter by GMC Domain**: See which domains you've covered
- **Filter by Type**: See all courses, teaching, etc.
- **View Total Hours**: Real-time calculation of filtered hours

**Tip**: Aim for balanced coverage across all 4 GMC domains!

## ğŸ“¥ Exporting for Appraisal

### Generate Your Portfolio
1. Click **Export** on the dashboard
2. Click **"Export Markdown"**
3. File downloads/shares as: `metanoia_appraisal_2025.md`

### What's Included
Your export contains:
- **Clinician Profile**: Name, GMC number
- **Appraisal Details**: Specialty, organisation, appraiser
- **Reflections by GMC Domain**: Organized into 4 sections
- **CPD by GMC Domain**: With hour totals per domain
- **Professional Formatting**: Ready to share with your appraiser

### Sharing Your Export
- **Email**: Attach the markdown file
- **Convert to PDF**: Open in any editor (Word, Google Docs) and save as PDF
- **Print**: For face-to-face appraisals

## âœ¨ Best Practices

### For High-Quality Reflections
1. **Be specific** about what happened
2. **Analyze deeply** - go beyond surface observations
3. **Commit to action** - concrete, measurable changes
4. **Select appropriate domains** - be honest about coverage
5. **Use tags** - make reflections easy to find later

### For Comprehensive CPD
1. **Record everything** - formal and informal learning
2. **Capture impact** - what difference did it make?
3. **Balance domains** - aim for coverage across all 4
4. **Track hours accurately** - for revalidation requirements
5. **Link to reflections** - show application of learning

### For Appraisal Preparation
1. **Start early** - don't leave until the last minute
2. **Review regularly** - update reflections as you learn more
3. **Check domain balance** - ensure coverage of all 4 areas
4. **Export in advance** - give yourself time to review
5. **Share with appraiser** - send export a week before meeting
